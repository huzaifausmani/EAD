Synchronous:
It means that you can execute one thing at a time.

Asynchronous:
It means that you can execute multiple things at a time.
You don't have to wait for other tasks while the execution of one task. 
Blocks the process till the operation completes.

Where to do asynchronous:
I/O bounds:
File Handling, DB access, requesting data from a network.

CPU bounds:
Performing an expensive calclations.
